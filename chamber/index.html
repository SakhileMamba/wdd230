Events

Matsapha Industrial Fair

Date: September 15-17, 2024

Experience the pulse of Eswatini's industrial heartland at the annual Matsapha Industrial Fair. This three-day event showcases the latest innovations and products from local manufacturers, featuring live demonstrations, networking opportunities, and seminars on sustainable industrial practices. Don't miss the chance to connect with industry leaders and discover the driving force behind Eswatini's economic growth.

Matsapha Cultural Food Festival

Date: November 5, 2024

Savor the flavors of Eswatini at the Matsapha Cultural Food Festival. This vibrant one-day event celebrates the rich culinary heritage of the region, featuring traditional Swazi dishes alongside modern fusion cuisine. Local chefs and food artisans will offer tastings, cooking demonstrations, and workshops. Enjoy live music and dance performances while indulging in the best of Swazi gastronomy.
